Lomiri

Why?

After Canonical terminated support for the Ubuntu Touch operating system, UBports stepped forward to resume development. The response from the community has been overwhelmingly positive, our user base is growing, and Ubuntu Touch is now available for use on a variety of mobile devices.

We feel that demand exists for using Lomiri on the desktop, and we hope to meet that demand.

Where?

Development is taking place on GitHub. You can also join our telegram groups: UBports and UBports Lomiri.

When?

Unlike on mobile devices, Lomiri is not yet ready for production use on the desktop. However, you can install a development preview on Ubuntu 18.04 to explore the current status. Visit our GitHub project to see what we are working on at the moment and where we could use your help.

What about Mir?

Development of the Mir display server (now compatible with Wayland) is continued by a very talented and motivated team at Canonical. You can contribute to their work directly upstream.
