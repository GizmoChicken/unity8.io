Unity8 - Ubports

Why?
After the announcement that Canonical will stop investing in Unity8, we stepped forward stating that we will continue development for Unity8. The reason why we will do that is that we believe in convergence, we believe convergence is the thing people want in the future, and now that desktop is slowly decreasing in users and mobile investing in mobile is a smart move in our opinion.
Where?
The development will take place at github: https://github.com/ubports/unity8
You can also join our telegram group: https://ubports.com/telegram
Builds
You can find builds here: http://ci.ubports.com/job/unity8/
The builds will eventually land into our system-image server ("https://system-image.ubports.com)
